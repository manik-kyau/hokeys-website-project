Frequently Asked Any Questions

A frequently asked questions (FAQ) list is often used in articles, websites, email lists,and online forums where common questions tend to recur.

What is hockey game?

Hockey is an outdoor game played between two teams of 11 players who use long curved sticks to hit a small ball and try to score goals.

What is the description of hockey sport?

What is ice hockey? Ice hockey is a game between two teams who wear skates and compete on an ice rink. Each team usually has six players.

How do you explain hockey?

The object of the game is to move a hockey puck from the center of the box out through a hole placed at the end of the box (known as the goal).

Is hockey a professional sport?

Ice hockey has been played at the Winter Olympics since 1924, and was in the 1920 Summer Olympics. North America's National Hockey League is the strongest professional.

What is a professional hockey player?

Primary duties: Professional athletes in hockey play for professional teams. They play hockey games in front of audiences and compete against other teams in their league.

How popular is professional hockey?

North America's preeminent ice hockey league ranks eighth in the list of most popular competitions, with the college football and basketball regular seasons.
